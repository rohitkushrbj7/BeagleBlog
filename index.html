The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. It was developed primarily for hunting hare (known as beagling). Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is also used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs worldwide. As a popular pet, it's known for its good temper and lack of inherited health problems.

Trivia

  4. Coloration: Tricolor or white in combination with tan/brown or brown/tan
  5. Life span: 12–15 years
